01 — Executive Summary

The Inflection Point

VeraData is an 18-year-old donor science company with deep domain expertise in the nonprofit space, a growing portfolio (Faircom, New River, Teal Media), and strong unit economics. The company is at an inflection point: light on product and technology leadership at a time when the business needs to shift from analytics services to a prescriptive analytics platform.

FractionalCPO recommends running a 4-week assessment to evaluate the state of VeraData's product org and opportunity. We will embed in your business to evaluate product maturity, go-to-market strategy, and operational readiness. This assessment gives VeraData and Beringer a clear-eyed view of what the market opportunity requires and a concrete plan to capture it.

02 — Why Now

Three Forcing Functions

01

Execution Risk Exposure

VeraData has already taken the first steps: a product manager is in place, an external development firm is engaged, and the team is building. But without embedded product and technology leadership, there's real execution risk. Leadership wants to de-risk this by building a stronger foundation, accelerating time to value, and setting the stage for a permanent product leader who can hit the ground running.

02

Disruptive Market Opportunity

AI has changed how products are built, distributed, and monetized. Productized service companies are rising up to claim market share. A mature data company like VeraData needs to seize the opportunity to move from services into scalable product offerings in order to stay competitive.

03

Maximizing Exit Value

VeraData has the opportunity to create a meaningful exit for Beringer in the next 18 months. Product transformation is the single biggest lever to increase enterprise value, reduce future dilution, and maximize investor returns. Leadership wants clarity on the right bets, the right team structure, and the right sequence to capture that value before the window closes.

04 — Scope

Three Core Pillars

This assessment takes 4 weeks with a 2-week buffer, covering three core pillars.

01

Product Strategy

We ground the entire engagement in a clear platform vision, rooted in where VeraData's donor science creates defensible advantage and what the prescriptive analytics opportunity actually requires to win. Without this, the roadmap has no foundation and the transformation risk stays high.

02

Execution Process

We pressure-test the organization's readiness to deliver across technology, product leadership, and org structure so the roadmap reflects reality. PE-backed transformations fail most often not from bad strategy but from org and technology gaps that weren't surfaced early enough.

03

Go-to-Market Motion

Distribution is where most platform transformations stall, suffering from execution and organizational alignment problems. We validate the revenue model and customer segments before commitments are made, because the strategy has to be executable or it's just a document on someone's desk.

Approach

Over four weeks, we conduct structured interviews across the full leadership team and a cross-section of customers, audit the technology and product foundation, and synthesize everything into a prioritized, board-ready roadmap. We move quickly without sacrificing rigor, and we connect every finding directly to a decision on product, technology, hiring, or go-to-market. We build the output to be acted on from day one.
